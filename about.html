About MacSetup.live

Our mission is to make setting up your Mac as simple as possible. No ads, no bundled software, just pure simplicity.

Our Story

Mac App Installer was born out of frustration with the complex process of setting up a new Mac. We wanted to create a tool that would make this process as simple as possible, inspired by Ninite for Windows but built specifically for the Mac ecosystem.

Our Mission

We believe that setting up your Mac should be simple, secure, and efficient. Our goal is to save you time and effort while ensuring you get your apps from official sources, without any bundled software or advertisements.

Security First

Security is our top priority. All apps are downloaded directly from official sources, and our installation scripts are open source and transparent. We never collect any personal data or track our users.
